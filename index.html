About ISKCON Mayapur

The Vision of Srila Prabhupada

ISKCON Mayapur was founded by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada with the vision of creating a spiritual hub where people from all over the world could come to learn about and experience the teachings of Lord Krishna. This holy site is regarded as the birthplace of Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, the proponent of the Bhakti movement.

The Grand Temple of Vedic Planetarium

The Temple of the Vedic Planetarium is the centerpiece of ISKCON Mayapur. It is a majestic structure that blends modern engineering with ancient Vedic wisdom. The temple is designed to inspire awe and reverence, featuring intricate carvings, a massive chandelier representing the universe, and state-of-the-art displays of Vedic cosmology.

Daily Spiritual Activities

The temple offers a range of daily activities such as Mangala Arati, kirtans, Bhagavad Gita discourses, and prasadam distribution. Visitors are encouraged to participate and immerse themselves in the spiritual atmosphere of the temple.

Community Outreach Programs

ISKCON Mayapur is also known for its extensive community outreach programs. These include free food distribution, educational initiatives, and healthcare services for the underprivileged. The temple's efforts reflect the teachings of compassion and service.

Eco-Friendly Living

The temple complex promotes sustainable and eco-friendly living. With organic farming, renewable energy initiatives, and waste management programs, ISKCON Mayapur serves as a model for harmonious living with nature.

The Spiritual Significance of Mayapur

Mayapur is considered one of the most sacred places in India. It is part of the Navadvipa Mandala, a cluster of nine islands symbolizing the petals of a lotus flower. The spiritual vibrations of this land attract thousands of devotees annually.

Festivals and Celebrations

The temple hosts numerous festivals throughout the year, including Gaura Purnima, Janmashtami, and Ratha Yatra. These events are celebrated with grandeur, drawing devotees and visitors from all over the world.

A Place of Global Harmony

ISKCON Mayapur stands as a beacon of global harmony, welcoming people from diverse backgrounds to unite under the banner of love and devotion to Lord Krishna. The temple's motto is "Simple living, high thinking."
